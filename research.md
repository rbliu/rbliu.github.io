<section class="section-card panel">
  <h2 class="section-title">Selected Directions</h2>
  <div class="timeline">
    <div class="timeline__item">
      <h3 class="timeline__title">Galaxy clusters and gravitational lensing</h3>
      <p class="timeline__meta">Scientific analysis</p>
      <p>
        I study galaxy clusters and related lensing phenomena, with an emphasis on
        extracting reliable scientific information from large observational datasets.
      </p>
    </div>
    <div class="timeline__item">
      <h3 class="timeline__title">WFST data processing pipeline development</h3>
      <p class="timeline__meta">Scientific software and infrastructure</p>
      <p>
        I contribute to development work related to the data processing pipeline for the
        Wide Field Survey Telescope (WFST), helping prepare analysis workflows for
        survey-scale imaging data.
      </p>
    </div>
    <div class="timeline__item">
      <h3 class="timeline__title">Asteroid detection</h3>
      <p class="timeline__meta">Survey applications</p>
      <p>
        I am also interested in asteroid detection problems, especially where survey
        data, image processing, and efficient detection strategies come together.
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
